Mini Ponies

Mini Ponies are little ponies for your website!

Looking to annoy the heck out of your visitors with the most adorable animated equines ever? Of course you are! Mini Ponies is like Desktop Ponies, or Browser Ponies, except it's approximately 20% cooler! It's been written from the ground up to be dead-simple to impliment on your own site using jQuery, and it tosses out those old .ini files in favor of snazzy, more modern JSON configuration. So what are you waiting for, silly? Let's PARTY!
